How to get there

We are so grateful that you are making the trip to Lancaster for our wedding celebration.

Planes

The closest airport is Harrisburg (MDT), which is about 40 minutes away from Lancaster. The Philadelphia airport (PHL) and Baltimore airport (BWI) are both an hour and 30 minute drive from Lancaster. Amtrak runs from the Philadelphia airport directly to Lancaster.

Trains

For those without cars and coming from the Northeast, Amtrak stops directly in Lancaster (LNC). If you are traveling from New York, the train goes directly to Lancaster and is a 2 hour 45 minute train ride. Yes and, there is UBER in Lancaster to help you get around once you get here!

Automobiles

If you are driving in, do remember that Lancaster is surrounded by Amish Farms and you may see an Amish buggy or two driving down the road. Please be careful when passing a buggy!
